Company abbreviations

GWR = Great Western Railway
L&NWR = London and North Western Railway
SVR = Severn Valley Railway, part of the GWR
WMD = Wellington & Market Drayton Line
SUR = Shropshire Union Railway

The lines

  • SVR (Coalport to Buildwas Junction)
  • L&NWR (Coalport to Hadley):
  • Coalport to Aqueduct
  • Aqueduct to Hollinswood
  • Oakengates to Hadley
  • GWR (Buildwas Junction to Madeley Junction)
  • L&NWR and GWR Joint Railway (Madeley Junction through Wellington)
  • GWR (Lightmoor Junction to Ketley Junction):
  • Lightmoor Junction to Horsehay
  • Horsehay to Ketley Junction
  • L&NWR Shropshire Union Line (Wellington to Newport)
  • Wellington and Market Drayton Railway
